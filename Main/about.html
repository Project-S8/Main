Intelligent News

Introduction


A news application is a big interactive database that tells a news story. Think of it like you would any other piece of journalism. It just uses software instead of words and pictures. By showing each reader data that is specific to them, a news app can help each reader understand a story in a way that’s personally meaningful to them. It can help a reader understand their personal connection to a broad national phenomenon, and help them attach what they know to what they don’t know, and thereby encourage a deep understanding of abstract concepts. We tend to build a comprehensive news platform that gives a complete solution to the customers by providing features like speech recognition, recommendation, Wishlist, predictions, etc.

Objective of the Project


The website provides a wide array of legit news that can be chosen from genuine websites. Once this website comes into use one can fetch news from multiple news sources in one platform.

There is a search engine provided to add the input text to browse and fetch the news from different sources. In addition, our website uses a speech recognition system to convert the voice to text and fetch the news. Importantly, searching for news online is challenging without a voice recognition system. So the best suggested solution for the same is to receive the speech on the desired target news and enable the system to grab the content from the genuine sources.

News about the stock market is crucial today. Many people in our nation currently invest in various stocks. In addition to delivering stock news, giving a prediction system makes our system a more advanced solution.

Recommended list- The system tries to learn the nature and interest of the user. The system then recommends a list of news cards that gains the attention of the client and will be close to what they were actually looking for.

Wishlist- Platform consists of a facility where the client can add a news article to their wishlist for future purposes. They can come and refer this list in future and avoid browsing again. The items listed can also be deleted if required.

Description


The news app system first requires the new user to visit the website. Once the user visits the website, they can search for news they want to visit, by using the voice command feature of the app. After the search operation completes, the user gets the image and description of the selected news. The user can also view the in-depth news of the selected news card for better guidance. Based on the location entered by the user, a list of the news in and around the area is also provided. A built-in chatbot is available to answer the simple queries of the user.

Users will also be able to see the suggested news based on their interest and location. Since the app uses voice commands to search for news it would be helpful for the blind as well. Each headline of the news is read so that the blind has the option to select the desired news of the chosen category.

Another feature is the recommendation feature that allows news readers to see news related to the searched ones. This helps them gain information on their area of interest.

Wishlist- Platform consists of a facility where the client can add a news article to their wishlist for future purposes. They can come and refer this list in future and avoid browsing again. The items listed can also be deleted if required.

Stock Market Prediction:-News about the stock market is crucial today. Many people in our nation currently invest in various stocks. In addition to delivering stock news, giving a prediction system makes our system a more advanced solution.

Subscription:- Client can purchase a premium plan to access features like predictor, save for later, newspaper subscription.

We also added the feature of expanding categories to provide the big picture of stories within a category. Likewise, we suggest a simpler and more straightforward accordion style organization for improving the genuinity of the knowledge that they gain.
